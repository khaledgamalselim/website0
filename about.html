About us

We are a friendly team of barbers and hair stylists with 15 years of experience in Egypt. We stand behind every hairdresser and best customer service. We have an absolute passion for creating beautiful hair that feels amazing.

We offer a wide range of services from haircuts and styling, colouring, highlights, foils, balayage and hair treatments. We also offer a wide variety of products, to make sure you look your best. We take pride in our customer service.

We are always happy to answer any questions you may have. We are also conveniently located in Egypt, so you can come and see us the way that suits you best.
